Project Features & Idea

The AI-Based Smart Allocation Engine is designed to streamline the internship allocation process for students and companies. By leveraging artificial intelligence, the system ensures optimal matches based on skills, preferences, and company requirements.

Key Features:

  • Student Registration: Students can create profiles, upload resumes, and select preferred internship locations.
  • Company Registration: Companies can post internship opportunities and track applications.
  • Smart Allocation: AI-powered algorithm matches students with suitable internship positions.
  • OTP Authentication: Secure login and registration via OTP verification.
  • Forgot Password Feature: Users can easily reset passwords using verified OTP.
  • Admin Control: Administrators can monitor registrations, manage users, and oversee allocations.
  • Transparent Dashboard: Both students and companies can view and track progress.

Our goal is to make internship allocation efficient, fair, and fully automated, saving time and effort for both students and companies.
