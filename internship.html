Internship Schemes

Our platform offers structured internship schemes designed to match students with companies efficiently. Here’s an overview of the main schemes:

Summer Internship

  • Duration: 6–8 weeks
  • Eligibility: Students in 2nd or 3rd year of study
  • Objective: Gain practical experience in relevant industry projects

Winter Internship Program

  • Duration: 4–6 weeks
  • Eligibility: Students in any academic year with prerequisite skills
  • Objective: Exposure to seasonal or short-term company projects

Research Internship

  • Duration: 3–6 months
  • Eligibility: Advanced students or research scholars
  • Objective: Contribute to research and development projects in collaboration with companies

Virtual Internship

  • Duration: Flexible (usually 4–12 weeks)
  • Eligibility: Students unable to relocate or work onsite
  • Objective: Remote skill-building and project experience using online collaboration tools

These internship schemes are integrated with our AI-Based Smart Allocation Engine to ensure students are matched with the best opportunities according to their skills and preferences.
