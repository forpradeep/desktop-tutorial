About AI-Based Smart Allocation Engine

The AI-Based Smart Allocation Engine is a powerful platform designed to efficiently match students with internships and companies based on skills, preferences, and availability. It leverages AI algorithms to optimize the allocation process, saving time for both students and organizations.

This system allows:

  • Students to register, upload resumes, and select preferred internship locations.
  • Companies to register, post internship requirements, and track applications.
  • Administrators to manage the platform, monitor activities, and ensure smooth operations.

Our goal is to make internship allocation smarter, faster, and more transparent using AI-powered automation.
